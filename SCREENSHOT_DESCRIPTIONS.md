## Character Count Reference

- **Minimum**: 2 characters
- **Maximum**: 80 characters (for short descriptions)
- **Recommended**: 38-40 characters (as requested)
- **Best Practice**: Keep it concise and feature-focused

---

## Tips for Screenshot Descriptions

1. **Be Specific**: Mention the feature shown in the screenshot
2. **Use Action Words**: "Create", "Download", "Share", "Customize"
3. **Highlight Benefits**: What users can do with the feature
4. **Keep It Short**: 38-40 characters is perfect for quick scanning
5. **Match the Screenshot**: Description should match what's visible

---

**Note**: Google Play Console allows up to 80 characters for screenshot descriptions, but shorter (38-40 chars) is better for mobile viewing.
